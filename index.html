Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply pairs, is a game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Develop Brain Power

Playing this game helps promote mental health and agility by stretching your memory capabilities. This is a game that people of all ages can play and pit their skills against one another. To level up you skills, play where you must match a card in all four suits to remove the cards from play.
